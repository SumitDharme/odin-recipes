Turkey Goulash

A turkey-goulash, a delicious dish, TASTE IT NOW!

Turkish goulash, or "Türlü," is a savory stew blending tender meat and fresh vegetables in a spiced tomato sauce, offering a taste of Turkey's culinary heritage.

ingredients
  • Dough
  • Spinach and Feta Cheese
  • Potato
  • Ground Meat (Beef or Lamb)
  • Cheese and Herbs
recipe instruction
  1. Prepare the dough by mixing flour, salt, warm water, and olive oil; divide it into balls.
  2. Choose a filling, prepare it (e.g., sauté spinach, cook ground meat), and roll out the dough..
  3. Place the filling on one half of the dough, fold, and seal it to make a half-moon shape.
  4. Cook the gözleme in a pan with olive oil until both sides are crispy and golden.
  5. Serve hot with yogurt and lemon wedges for a delicious Turkish treat.

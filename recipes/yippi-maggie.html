Yummy Yippi noodles recipe!

ingridents
  • Yippee Noodles (any flavor of your choice)
  • Water
  • cooking oil(optional)
  • Vegetables or protein (e.g., sliced vegetables, tofu, chicken, or shrimp) - optional for added
  • Seasoning packet (included with Yippee Noodles)
recipe!
  1. Boil a pot of water (typically 2 cups per serving) and add the Yippee Noodles once the water is boiling. Cook the noodles according to the package instructions, usually for about 2-3 minutes or until they become tender but not mushy.
  2. While the noodles are cooking, you can optionally heat a small amount of cooking oil in a separate pan and stir-fry your choice of vegetables or protein until they're cooked to your liking. Set them aside.
  3. Once the noodles are cooked, drain them using a strainer or by simply pouring out the water. You can rinse them with cold water to stop the cooking process and remove excess starch if desired.
